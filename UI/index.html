SunWork Renewable Energy Projects is a non-profit in California's San Francisco Bay Area and Central Coast that installs solar electricity systems on small-energy-footprint homes with the help of trained volunteers.

ABOUT

SunWork's innovative model lets it deliver solar power to homeowners with already modest electricity use (and correspondingly low electricity bills) at a third less than the conventional cost, allowing more people to make the switch to solar and enabling broader adoption of this clean, safe, and renewable alternative to the sources of energy that contribute to climate change.

SunWork is deeply committed to creating a safe, energetic, enjoyable, and meaningful experience for its volunteers, and to achieving the highest standard of quality and convenience for its customers.

 

SunWork also installs solar systems

for small non-profit organizations.
